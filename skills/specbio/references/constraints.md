# Ellieworld Physical Constraints

Hard physical/chemical constraints that won't change. For biology, read the project docs directly.

## Planet

- Surface gravity: 1.95g (19.14 m/s²)
- Atmosphere: 3,100 hPa, 65% N₂, 21% O₂, 12.9% CO₂
- Mean surface temp: 20.4°C
- Tidally locked, ~2.69-day orbit around 1968 K brown dwarf
- No visible light. All stellar flux is near/mid-IR
- No UV (10⁻⁵ solar)
- Cloud cover: ~79%
- Tidal heating: 20.6 W/m² (~230× Earth's geothermal)
- Tectonics: heat-pipe regime

Photic zone ~100× shallower than Earth's.

## Ocean

- Mixed layer: 700 m, violently convective
- No sea ice
- No stratification except locally under dense surface mats
- Hydrostatic pressure at 700 m: ~137 atm (gas vesicles collapse at 3-10 atm)

## Land

- Volcanic terrain: fresh lava flows, quiescent plains, calderas, lava tubes
- Heat-pipe tectonics — no plate tectonics, no continents in the Earth sense
- Quiescent land surfaces can persist 70+ Myr (younger than Earth cratons but long-lived)
- Caldera lakes as transitional habitats

## Key Biomaterial Chemistry

Dissolved silicic acid abundant (volcanic world). Iron and sulfur abundant (hydrothermal). Calcium carbonate, chitin, cellulose are Earth-specific and absent here.
